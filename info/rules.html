Rules

Rules of conduct go here. As opposed to game rules (in mechanics) these should be behavioral rules. Here are sample rules you can use:

All players agree to adhere to the rules outlined in this page. Failure to heed warnings of misconduct will lead to removal from the group.

Etiquette

  1. Communicate! If you're in the middle of a roleplay thread and you lose steam or life gets busy, that's fine! Just let your partner know, or ask them if they're okay with summarizing or headcanoning.
  2. Please contact a group moderator if someone is breaking the rules. The moderator will issue a warning to the offender, anonymously.
  3. Do not flame or insult other members, whether in regards to characters or gameplay. Please do not insult others’ creative ability or ability to play games!
  4. Please do not conflate members’ beliefs with characters’ actions. Please do not use your character to vent personal frustrations on another player. Keep ic and ooc separation in mind.
  5. No Metagaming: do not make your character privvy to information they would not ic-ly know. When in doubt, ask the source of the information, whether this is fellow players or the mods.
  6. No Powerplaying: Your character should not overwhelmingly beat others in all situations. Discuss outcomes with your peers or feel free to use RNG if you don’t trust yourself (at the same time, dice rolls are not word of god and you can ignore a dice roll even after you’ve made it. Sometimes you only get ideas on what you want to happen after you’ve rolled).
  7. No Sub-talk: no venting on social media about other members. Do not post about other members where other members or uninvolved parties can see– even if names are not mentioned.
  8. No Guilt Tripping: do not put one’s self down to garner sympathy and attention, or to control the decisions of others. This includes insulting one’s own art ie. “I’m not as good at drawing as you”, one’s own ability to solve puzzles ie. “I’m so stupid, I don’t get it”, or pressuring others into interactions ie. “I wish someone would ship with my character but maybe they’re too unlikeable.”
  9. No Oversharing: Do not user the server to share heavy private information such as work life, health issues, family issues, etc which may trigger others or cause them to feel the need to take on the burden of your emotional issues ie. “I’m being kicked out of my house today” “my brother died” “I want to quit my job but I can’t.”
  10. No Overshadowing: Avoid abruptly changing the flow of conversation or interrupting an ongoing conversation– either wait for the conversation to reach its natural end, or use another related channel. If a character or player is having their big moment, do not try to steal the spotlight. Please be mindful of posting your own art or content seconds after someone else posts without acknowledging their work at all.
  11. Please be mindful of squicks/triggers of your peers, and tag things/spoiler them appropriately. Better safe than sorry.
  12. Please keep all NSFW out of the server. Do not share NSFW artwork in the server and do not roleplay NSFW in the server. You are allowed to post NSFW on your own spaces (such as social media, toyhou.se, etc) and you are allowed to RP ‘fade to blacks’ aka moving to DM’s for NSFW RP. Jokes are fine, but please avoid explicit topics. Do not pressure others into ERP

Penalties

  1. For minor offenses, moderators may issue warnings.
  2. Penalties for minor offenses may include temporary bans from participation in any or all group activities including but not restricted to:
    • Access to the group Discord
    • Events
    • Character Creation
  3. Major offenses or repeated minor offenses will result in an immediate ban and blacklist.
  4. Characters of blacklisted individuals will be removed from the group.
These rules may be subject to change.
